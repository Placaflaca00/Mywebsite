Who I Am

I am a driven software engineering student entering my third year, with a passion for programming and an eagerness to learn new languages and technologies. My skillset includes proficiency in Python, HTML, CSS, and currently diving into Java. In the summer of 2022-2023, I start a small business creating personalized images using AI. In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, I am an avid fitness enthusiast who hits the gym twice a day, dedicating one session to fitness and the other to my love for boxing. I find boxing to be not only a great form of exercise but also a powerful way to channel energy and stay motivated. My ultimate goal is to use my education and expanding knowledge to create something meaningful that can generate income for me. I believe that it's not about the money, it's about sending a message.
